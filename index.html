Why XTC?

MDMA‐assisted psychotherapy could be useful in the treatment of disorders associated with attachment insecurities, including PTSD, depression, anxiety disorders, obsessive‐compulsive disorder, suicidality, substance use disorders, and eating disorders. With piXelTC we want to bring attention to the topic and with a part of the raised funds we want to support researchers!

The Cardano Pushers

Bringing drug policy to the blockchain

Concept

The COVID-19 pandemic has seen devastating impacts, inducing a wide range of psychological responses, including fear, anxiety, traumatic stress, and depression. Disorders for which established drugs have insufficient effect. MDMA (and other psychedelics) could be a game changer.

Research

MDMA-assisted psychotherapy could help heal the psychological and emotional damage caused by sexual assault, war, violent crime, and other traumas. But there is not too much research yet. That's why we want to contribute a part.

Illustration

Low Mint Price

We decided to set the mint price relatively low so that it the barrier as low as possible while still be able to collect funds which we can donate.
